MACHANICAL REMOVAL OF DEPOSITS

Our Mechanical Removal of Deposits service is designed to effectively eliminate the most persistent and tenacious deposits on your tiled surfaces. Over time, tiles can accumulate various types of deposits, including limescale, mold, grime, and even rust, which not only detract from their appearance but can also cause damage if left untreated.

We utilize state-of-the-art mechanical equipment and specialized cleaning agents that are resilient to deposits but gentle on your tiles. This approach ensures a thorough clean without risking damage to the tile's surface. Our team of experts is trained to work with a variety of tile materials, guaranteeing that we can restore the original luster and beauty of your tiled areas regardless of their condition or type.

UNDERFLOOR HEATING INSTALLATION

Underfloor heating systems represent a paradigm shift in the way spaces are warmed, offering an innovative, efficient, and uniform heating solution. These systems, which can be either electric or hydronic, are installed beneath the flooring to provide consistent, radiant heat across the entire surface area. The beauty of underfloor heating lies in its ability to distribute heat evenly, eliminating cold spots and creating a more comfortable living environment.

At Master Builders VS LTD, we are experts in the installation and integration of underfloor heating systems. Our approach is grounded in a deep understanding of these systems' nuances, ensuring that every installation is tailored to the unique needs of each space. We take pride in our ability to work with a variety of flooring types, ensuring that the underfloor heating system functions optimally without compromising the integrity or aesthetic of your flooring.

WET ROOMS AND TANKS/HYDRO INSULATION

Wet rooms and tanks, as well as hydro insulation, are specialized services that require expertise and precision. At Master Builders VS LTD, we excel in creating functional and stylish wet rooms, ensuring that every aspect of the room is waterproof and efficiently designed. Our team is skilled in the installation of waterproof tanks and hydro insulation systems that prevent any water leakage or damage, making your space safe and enduring.

The process involves meticulous planning and execution, utilizing high-quality materials and advanced techniques.
